Credit & debit cards

We charge a percentage-based fee each time you accept a credit or debit card payment. The price is the same for all major cards. There's no additional fee for international cards, failed charges, or refunds.

ACH and Bitcoin payments

ACH and Bitcoin fees are capped at $5 -- payments above $625 cost $5. We also provide tools to verify customers' bank acccounts at no additional cost. We charge $4 for failed ACH payments.
